“Jorge has commissioned himself bravely to accomplish various studies simultaneously, being a non-native speaker to this education. The consequences of these choices made an extended study course at PERK a favourable option.

The international design scene belongs to his basic resources, his visionary almost utopian imagination of spaces propels his designs to a next level in the game. The harass of practical issues will unceasingly embarass him, but he is armed with a maniacal inspiration, and a natural sense of elegance and grandeur.

This spirit will definitely outlive the prosaic realities of the professional challenges! The graduation project demonstrated his competences in the uplifting of a concrete moloch through an urban utopian, tasteful and yet feasible spatial intervention!

The mix of theatrical and architectural qualities have resulted here in an exciting plan, effectively visualised, revealing a fascinating designer’s mind!”

 

Carla Boomkens, tutor.
